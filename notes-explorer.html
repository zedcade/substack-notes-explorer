2Run it on Substack

Go to substack.com/@yourhandle — your own profile page specifically (not the home feed, not any other page) — and make sure you're logged in. Click the bookmark you just saved. A small progress panel appears in the bottom-right corner of the page — leave the tab open until it says "Done." A JSON file will download automatically when it finishes. (This takes a while for accounts with lots of notes. NOTES EXPLORER fetches 6 notes in parallel and auto-saves every 50 — if interrupted, re-clicking the bookmark offers to resume where it left off.)
